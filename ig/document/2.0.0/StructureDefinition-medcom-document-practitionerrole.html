Resource Profile: MedComDocumentPractitionerRole

Official URL: http://medcomfhir.dk/ig/document/StructureDefinition/medcom-document-practitionerrole Version: 2.0.0
Active as of 2026-01-14 Realm: Computable Name: MedComDocumentPractitionerRole

Document PractitionerRole resource used to describe the role of a healthcare professional or another actor involved in citizen or patient care.

Scope and usage

The MedComDocumentCareTeam profile describes the resource that shall be used in MedCom FHIR Documents containing CareTeams. It is interited from the MedComCorePractitionerRole profile, and has narrowed down which profile that can be referenced.

Usages:

  • Refer to this Profile: MedComContainedDocumentReference, MedComDocumentComposition and MedComDocumentObservation
  • Examples for this Profile: PractitionerRole/bb6fa4e1-f8b1-4bf4-b77e-bb03b2cc9820

You can also check for usages in the FHIR IG Statistics
